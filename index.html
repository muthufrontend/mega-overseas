Choose Your Destination

Popular Study Destinations

We place students in world-renowned universities across the globe. Hover over any country card to explore university facts.

USA

Top STEM Hub & Post-Study Work Option

Study in USA

  • Top Universities: Harvard, MIT, Stanford, UC Berkeley
  • Post-Study Visa: Up to 3 Years (STEM OPT extension)
  • Intakes: Fall (Sept), Spring (Jan)
  • Key Benefit: Leading research facilities and high salaries

United Kingdom

1-Year Master's & 2-Year Post-Study Visa

Study in UK

  • Top Universities: Oxford, Cambridge, Imperial, UCL
  • Post-Study Visa: 2 Years (Graduate Route)
  • Intakes: Sep, Jan, May
  • Key Benefit: Shorter degree duration saves costs

Canada

Top Choice for Permanent Residency (PR)

Study in Canada

  • Top Universities: Toronto, UBC, McGill, Waterloo
  • Post-Study Visa: Up to 3 Years PGWP
  • Intakes: Sep, Jan, May
  • Key Benefit: Direct pathways to permanent residency

Australia

High Wages & Excellent Quality of Life

Study in Australia

  • Top Universities: Melbourne, Sydney, ANU, UNSW
  • Post-Study Visa: 2 to 5 Years work rights
  • Intakes: Feb, July, Nov
  • Key Benefit: High minimum wages & sunshine weather

Germany

Tuition-Free Public Universities

Study in Germany

  • Top Universities: TU Munich, LMU Munich, Heidelberg
  • Post-Study Visa: 18 Months stay-back period
  • Intakes: Winter (Oct), Summer (April)
  • Key Benefit: Free education with high job opportunities

New Zealand

Safe, Peaceful & 3-Year Post Study Visa

Study in New Zealand

  • Top Universities: Auckland, Otago, Canterbury
  • Post-Study Visa: Up to 3 Years work rights
  • Intakes: Feb, July
  • Key Benefit: Peaceful lifestyle with robust support services

Ireland & Europe

Fast-Growing Tech Hubs & Schengen Access

Study in Ireland & Europe

  • Top Universities: Trinity College Dublin, UCD, Paris-Saclay
  • Post-Study Visa: 2 Years (Ireland) stay back
  • Intakes: Sept, Jan
  • Key Benefit: Access to global tech and business headquarters
